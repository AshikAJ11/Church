Our Story

Founded in 1952, St.Antony'S Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for over 70 years. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational church family.

Our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world.

We believe in the power of the Gospel to transform lives and communities. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking to deepen your relationship with Christ, you're welcome here.

Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Our children's ministry provides a safe, fun environment where kids can learn about Jesus at their own level. We offer Sunday School classes for all ages, Vacation Bible School each summer, and special events throughout the year.

Meeting Times: Sundays at 10:00 AM (during service) | Wednesdays at 6:30 PM

Youth Ministry

Our youth group is designed to help teenagers grow in their faith while building meaningful relationships. We meet weekly for games, worship, and Bible study, plus special events like retreats and mission trips.
