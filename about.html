Our Story & Mission

Discover the heart behind St. Antony's Church

Our History

1952 - Humble Beginnings

St. Antony's Church was founded by Rev. Johnathan Grace and 12 faithful members in a small rented hall. Our first service had just 18 attendees, but the vision was large - to create a Christ-centered community that would transform lives.

1965 - First Building

After 13 years of growth, we dedicated our first church building on Faith Avenue. The construction was largely done by volunteer labor from congregation members, a testament to our community spirit.

1980 - Community Outreach

We launched our first major outreach program - a food pantry that served over 200 families weekly. This marked the beginning of our deep commitment to serving the practical needs of our community.

1995 - Education Wing

Added our education wing to accommodate growing Sunday School programs and Bible study groups. This expansion allowed us to triple our children's ministry capacity.

2010 - Modern Sanctuary

Completed our current sanctuary building, featuring state-of-the-art facilities while preserving the historic charm of our original structure. The dedication service was attended by over 1,000 people.

Present Day - Flourishing Community

Today, St. Antony's Church serves over 2,000 members with multiple worship services, 12 active ministries, and community programs that impact thousands annually. Our legacy continues to grow.

Our Mission & Values

Christ-Centered Worship

We exist to glorify God through authentic worship that celebrates Jesus Christ as Lord. Our services blend traditional and contemporary elements to engage all generations.

Community Transformation

We're committed to being God's hands and feet in our city through practical service, social justice initiatives, and partnerships with local organizations.

Discipleship & Growth

We provide pathways for spiritual growth at every life stage through small groups, Bible studies, and mentoring relationships that foster deep faith.
